The Digital Metabolism Test

Section 1: Who Are You?

How many digital devices have you owned in your life?


How often do you check your phone in a day?


If you lost your phone tomorrow how would you feel?


Do you consider yourself a consumer or a user?

Have you ever wanted to go viral?

How many toxic pollutants is your phone made with?


Section 2: Where Are You?

How many data centers does your country have?

How many miles of intercontinental telecommunications cables in the world?

Is your tech running on any clean energy?

Is your data secure?

If you said yes to the last question, are you sure?


Where would you map yourself on a spectrum between physical and digital?

Do you know where your phone's rare earth elements came from?

Section 3: When Are You?

How does the concept of the "internet of things" make you feel?


How does the concept of cyborgs make you feel?

How does the concept of atmospheric media make you feel?

How does the concept of locative media make you feel?

Do you think we should care more about the stuff that makes up our technology?
